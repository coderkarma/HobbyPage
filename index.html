Train, Eat healthy and stay Fit

Training and staying physically fit is my hobby, I love working out even it is for a short period of time because it helps me to focus more and keeps me motivated to live healthy and better life.

  • Run and have fun

    I like running. It's fun and it has great benefits for health. It improves our cardiovascular and keeps us healthy.

  • Nutrition

    I think Nutrition is key whether to transform our body or to stay healthy. It plays a vital role in life and well being.

  • Train Smart

    I like weight training. it feels so good when we train intensely and sweat it out. Weight training has many benefits to health.

Get stronger everyday.

No matter what we do in our life, I feel being human we should always keep learning and strive for success. Some people say, fitness is just to go to the gym and do weight training but that is not all about. It's all about taking care of our body and living a healthy life. It's a choice and decision of individuals how we want to live life.

Stay Motivated

I know it's tough to stay motivated all time. We have ups and downs in life, we must admit that that's a reality but What I've learned in my life so far whether when it comes to training, programming or learning new things, never give up in life. Always keep learning and stay humble and take care of yourself.

So always stay motivated even when you are going through hard times.

Chase your goal

It doesn't matter what goals you have in life, whether it is fitness, education or financial, go for it. Have that hunger, drive and passion. Work bit by bit. As there is good saying "Rome wasn't built in a day".
